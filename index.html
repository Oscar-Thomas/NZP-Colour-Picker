Our Colours

Our colours are designed to capture the spirit of NZ Police. They are instantly recognisable visual anchors, derived from traditional associations with Police; such as our uniforms and patrol car lights. They also draw on the New Zealand flag, our cultural history, the fact we're an island nation, and Police's nature as a highly visible organisation.
